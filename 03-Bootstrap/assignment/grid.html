Thriller

This is the focal point of your container garden. Thrillers are tall, striking plants that stand out due to their height, structure, or bold colors. They are often placed in the center or back of the container. Examples include ornamental grasses, tall flowering plants like cannas, or dramatic foliage plants like elephant ears.

Filler

Fillers are medium-height plants that fill the space between the Thriller and the Spillers. They provide volume and help to tie the whole arrangement together. Fillers are typically chosen for their ability to blend well with both the Thrillers and Spillers, often featuring complementary colors or textures. Examples include petunias, geraniums, or coleus.

More
Spiller

Spillers are trailing or cascading plants that hang over the edges of the container, softening its edges and adding a flowing, dynamic element to the design. They are usually placed around the outer edges of the container so their foliage or flowers can spill over the sides. Examples include trailing ivy, sweet potato vine, or creeping jenny.
