Thrillers

Thrillers are tall plants that provide a focal point. They are typically placed in the center or back of the container. Examples include ornamental grasses, tall flowering plants like cannas, or foliage plants like ferns (shown).

More
Fillers

Fillers are medium-height plants that fill the space between the thriller and the spillers. Fillers compliment the thriller and the spillers, with complementary colors and textures. Examples include begonias, petunias, impatiens, and violas.

More
Spillers

Spillers are cascading plants that hang over the edges of the container. They add a dramatic element and are usually placed around the outer edges, so they can spill over the sides. Examples include trailing ivy, sweet potato vine, and creeping jenny (shown).
